ahmii recipie for the best cinnamon rolls

ingredients

for the dough

  • 3 cup of warm milk
  • 2 teaspon yeast
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus one egg yolk
  • one cup butter
  • 3 cup bread floor

for the filling

  • 2 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 table spoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instruction

  1. mix the mix with yeast sugar and egg
  2. melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. add the floor and mix until combined into dough
  4. knead the dough for 10 mint
  5. transfer the dough to a large bowl and wrap it with a plastic bag
